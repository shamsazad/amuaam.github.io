Up-coming Event: EID Celebration

5:00 PM — 9:00 PM 8736, Rue Riviera, Brossard

Alumini meet 2019

This was the first alumini meet after several years of desolation. To conclude, this event marked the bicentennial of a great man who was once opposed tooth and nail by his own community while he built a college that was later to become Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) whose alumni went on to make numerous contributions to the entire World and as we had a great time meeting with each other, it would be important to ponder over this question: Where would we all be today had not AMU touched our lives in some way?
